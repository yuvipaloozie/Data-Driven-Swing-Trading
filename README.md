# A ML-Based Approach to Swing Trading
Swing trading is a style of investing that aims to capitalize on the longer terms swings of stock, typically those that are more volatile, in an effort to increase returns relative to both the market and the simply buy-and-hold of the underlying asset. The typical timeframe for these swings can range from days to weeks, and are influenced by a variety of factors (technical, fundamental, sentiment, etc). While rule-based/mathematical models have proven effective in some contexts, this problem offers a unique opportunity to leverage machine learning/deep learning based approaches. This repo covers several "levels" of models attempting to utilize a "black-box" approach in order to capture complex nonlinearities in the movement of a stock in order to produce a reliable strategy
